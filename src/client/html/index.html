Jump King .io

Reach the top with minimum jumps!

How to Play:

  • 🎮 Use Arrow Keys or A/D to move left/right
  • ⬆️ Hold SPACE to charge jump, release to jump
  • 🧊 Ice tiles - You slide!
  • ❄️ Snow tiles - Jump only, no walking
  • 🔻 Slope tiles - You slide down uncontrollably
  • 🏆 Gold tiles - Finish line!
